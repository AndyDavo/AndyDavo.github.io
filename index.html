The Solution

Automated Reconciliation

Process millions of transactions daily across multiple payment providers. Our automated system matches and reconciles payments as frequently as daily, eliminating backlog and minimising errors.

Rapid Exception Handling

Smart detection and routing of exceptions ensures rapid resolution. Our system identifies, categorises, and manages discrepancies automatically, maintaining processing efficiency.

Multi-Currency Support

Handle transactions across multiple fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies effortlessly. Our platform automatically manages exchange rates and reconciles across all currency types in real-time.

Seamless Integration

Connect to your existing systems in weeks, not months. Our cloud-native platform integrates effortlessly with your current infrastructure and payment providers.
